Oslo Queens


Oslo Knockers was established on the 1st of January 2020. It was
founded by Enock Ladu on his pursue of creating the best soccer
team in Norway. The club's name was originally Oslo Kickers but
Ladu realized it would not meet the visions he had for the team.
The club originally started with one keeper, three players and
a club manager. Through hard work and persistent teamwork we
managed to climb through the ranks and enter Eliteserien fairly
quickly. We expanded the team to now have 20 players, 3 keepers
and 2 managers, one being an assistant. We also managed to get
our own homeground at Ullevål Stadium. The vision of Oslo Knockers
is to knock at any door being a hindrance, enter that door and
score our way through. We believe in hard work, integrity,
teamwork and most importantly love for our teammates.
